Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Machine Learning

Our Mission: Precision and Accessibility

**Yogasthali** (meaning 'place of yoga') is a passion project built on the principle that precise alignment should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We aim to **democratize constructive yoga feedback**, translating the intricate wisdom of classical *asanas* into immediate, visual guidance. Whether you're a complete beginner finding your foundation or an experienced practitioner refining a deep posture, our goal is to serve as your patient, digital instructor, helping you realize the full, safe potential of your practice.

— Practice with awareness, grow with precision.

The Technology Stack

Hey there! Welcome to Yogasthali, developed in 2025.

We created Yogasthali inspired by various Machine Learning models for visual recognition. This is our first Machine Learning model and our first attempt at Web Development. we have loved (almost) every second of this project and we have learnt more than we could have imagined.

Yogasthali uses Pose.Net, developed by Google’s TensorFlow, and ml5.js, a JavaScript library for Machine Learning on the browser. Most of our learning was following The Coding Train’s YouTube playlist on Pose Estimation.
